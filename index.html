ABUOT US

Tesla is the Electronics Society of IIIT-Allahabad which operates under GeekHaven, The Technical Society of IIIT-Allahabad.
Its a dedicated team of two co-ordinators and five members which conducts workshops for freshmen and sophomores.
Besides providing hands-on experience on Arduino to the first years, we also deal with other aspects of Hardware and Software.
We also conduct various events throughout the year to keep the students motivated.
