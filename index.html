ABOUT US

Tesla, the Electronics Society of IIITA, is a group of dedicated 2nd and 3rd year
students with the goal of involving more and more students in the fun of side ECE branch.
While a major part of GeekHaven will keep you glued to your laptops only, Tesla is the sole wing
where you gain hands-on experience with actual hardware including Arduino. From beginner's workshops to the flagship event Topbot,
Tesla takes you to a journey where you learn, apply and witness your ideas turn into reality.
From creating line-follower robots, to testing your decision making skills at Wolf of 2311, from innovating with Arduino to creating your own Jarvis,
Tesla makes sure that you never get bored, that you always learn something new,
and that in the end, you have much to contribute and share with all!

EVENTS AND WORKSHOPS

WORKSHOPS

Workshops providing hands-on training with Arduino micro-controller,
IoT and Robotics are conducted throughout the year.

TOPBOT

Our flagship Robotics event organized during Aparoksha,
open for interested stdents across all years.

WOLF OF 2311

The quiz cum problem-solving event of Aparoksha
testing you on all the electronics and coding knowledge you got.

TRI-HACKER CUP

A four round team event, where first-years are tested
on various skills and areas of development.

PRO-ARDUINO

An online event organized on circuits.io
where you will be tested on your problem-solving skills with Arduino.

ELECTROBUZZ

Make IC 741 and IC 555 dance to your bits
in this exciting event.
